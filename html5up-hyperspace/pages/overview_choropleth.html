District Choropleth

Comparing 2018 to 2022, every NYC school district experienced a decrease in attendance. That decrease, however, was not spread across the city evenly. Below, we can see that districts in Harlem and deeper in Queens and Brooklyn experienced the largest decrease in attendance.

You can use the icons to the right of the choropleth to inspect different sections of the city in greater detail.
